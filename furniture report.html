Vietnam's Strategic Advantage in the Global Supply Chain

1. High Tariff Shield

FDI manufacturers in Vietnam enjoy complete tariff mitigation under key trade pacts (EVFTA, CPTPP) and avoid the high US anti-dumping duties faced by other competitors.

2. Port Hub Ecosystem

Industrial clusters in Binh Duong and Dong Nai have direct deep-water port access (Cat Lai, Cai Mep) reducing local transit and logistics costs.

3. Raw Material Security

Abundant local reserves of FSC-certified rubberwood, acacia, and eucalyptus alongside a robust import network for European oak/ash.

4. Highly Skilled Labor

Vietnam boasts world-renowned mastery in intricate carving, solid wood joining, and high-precision CNC manufacturing specialized for premium woodcraft.
