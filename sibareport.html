3. Conversion Funnel Analysis

For precision engineering and OEM fabrication, order values are high, requiring a balanced funnel. Here is a fair projection of conversion performance:

A. Offline Exhibitions (MTA Vietnam / Vinamac Expo)

  • Total Reach: ~2,000 professional visitors per booth.
  • Inquiry Conversion: 5% (100 business cards collected).
  • Order Conversion: 1-2 actual contracts, often localized within Vietnam or neighboring regions.

B. SEO Website (Siba.com.vn Global)

  • Global Traffic: ~1,000 targeted visits/month from relevant industrial keywords.
  • Inquiry Conversion: 1% (120 inquiries/year).
  • Order Conversion: 2-4 contracts per year, typically after 6-12 months of consistent content investment.

C. Alibaba.com (Guarantee Basic Package)

  • Visibility (Impressions): ~8,000 targeted B2B buyers reached per month.
  • VIP Business Opportunities: Guaranteed 25+ high-intent inquiries from verified global buyers.
  • Request for Quotation (RFQ): 480 proactive bids/year. Siba directly approaches buyers who are currently searching for sheet metal solutions.
  • Projected Conversion: With 505 total opportunities (RFQ + VIP Leads), a conservative 0.8% - 1.2% conversion rate results in 4 - 6 international export contracts per year.
Note: These figures are based on the average performance of Vietnamese mechanical manufacturers on the platform. Actual results depend on Siba’s speed of response and technical quotation quality.
